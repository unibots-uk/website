UniBots UK 2022 is an autonomous robotics competition which you can enter for free!

Come and learn about robotics with students from other UK universities and compete against them.
The subject of the competition will be announced in the Autumn term of 2021 and you will have till Easter to perfect your design.

Throughout this time we will support you and you will be able to talk to and learn from other students. This is an opportunity to put theory into practice and most importantly have fun playing with robots!
