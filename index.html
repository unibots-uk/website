Subsidies

We will offer each team a £100 subsidy for any incurred expense! To qualify, teams should use Fusion360 to design their robot, and have a moving robot on the competition day.

Competition Details

Date: Sat, 25th Mar 2023
Time: 1000-1700
Venue: Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge, Trumpington St, Cambridge CB2 1PZ, United Kingdom

Intermediate-level Competition

We designed the competition to allow anyone who has elementary experience with robotics to be able to build and compete.

Our arena pits 4 robots against each other, allowing more experienced competitors to showcase their strategies as well.

Overall, our competition should be great fun for competitors with any level of experience!

Competition Day

The competition will be held at the University of Cambridge, right after term ends for most universities.

The competition is a great way to meet other students that are as passionate about robotics as you are.

Not to mention, it's also a good excuse to visit Cambridge!

If all this sounds fun, sign up for our competition! Sign-ups close on 31st Jan so that we can order shirts and other swag. However, do sign up early as we have a limited budget for subsidies. (estimated first ~20 teams)
